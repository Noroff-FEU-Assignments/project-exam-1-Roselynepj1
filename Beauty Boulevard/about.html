Below are some lines about me and the Beauty Boulevard's origin

My name is Roselyne, a creative lady with a positive attitude towards living. Having worked for several years in the skincare industry and seeing how both the adolescents and adults struggle with different skin conditions, together with a team of experienced dermatologists, I decided to establish the Beauty Boulevard. A company where people from different walks of life do not only get skin care products, but also receive advice on how to take good care of their skin. I believe that hair care and skin care doesn't have to cost a fortune. I hope you enjoy and come back here often for useful skin care advice. Join us on this wellness journey as we help millions of people regain their confidence, improve their self-esteem and self worth and help them live happily, free of skin ailments We also provide a weekly news later that include our new products on the market and useful skin care tips that will change your life.
